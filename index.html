Projects

Engineering builds

01

Flexible Haptic Module — ERF

Designed and fabricated a flexible haptic module combining silicone-based materials with Electro-Rheological Fluid. Tested prototypes using a Dynamic Mechanical Analyzer (DMA) at Miami's Smart Materials Lab.

02

Tuned Vibration Absorber

Designing an ERF-based tuned vibration absorber with a variable damping coefficient, enabling it to match and suppress different resonant frequencies dynamically. Conference paper in preparation.

03

CBRNE Mask-Cleaning System

Modified and upgraded a mask-cleaning system for military and first-responder CBRNE equipment at AGarvey LLC. Improved clean quality, reduced overheating tendency, and presented the product at military conferences including CBOA.

04

Semi-Automated Aquaponic Farm

TSA 2022 National Champion. Led a team of 6 to design and build a semi-automated aquaponic farm in response to the prompt "Restore or improve urban infrastructure." Improved system efficiency from 30% to 90% across three testing phases.

05

PLC Automated Assembly Line Tool

Developed a pneumatic system with PLC ladder logic at The Fischer Group, allowing line workers to replace materials without halting production — saving up to 2 minutes per replacement cycle.

06

Manufacturing Clamps & Oil Distribution Systems

Collaborated with fellow interns to design and fabricate manufacturing fixtures and oil distribution systems. Gained proficiency in CAM software and subtractive manufacturing with metals.
